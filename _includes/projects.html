Advanced American Options Pricing Chatbot

Advanced American Options Pricing Chatbot

Challenge:
• Volatility clustering: Traditional models fail to capture rapid changes in volatility, reducing prediction accuracy.
• Dynamic market regimes: Inability to adapt to shifting market conditions leads to inconsistent pricing outcomes.
• Early exercise probabilities: Difficulty in modeling optimal early exercise behavior limits the practical usability of traditional approaches.

Solution:
• Neural Network-based pricing platform: Combined traditional pricing models with trader-intuition features and liquidity metrics to enhance decision-making.
• Synthetic data generation engine: Simulated complex market scenarios to improve model robustness and adaptability.
• Scalable deployment on Google Cloud Platform: Utilized Vertex AI for scalable training and BigQuery for efficient data management.
• NLP-based querying tool: Enabled real-time scenario analysis and model comparison to support dynamic strategy adjustments.

Outcome:
• 35% improvement in pricing accuracy: Achieved better performance over traditional binomial models.
• Dynamic scenario analysis: Empowered traders to adjust key market parameters and analyze outcomes in real time.
• Enhanced model reliability: Synthetic data ensured robustness across varied market conditions, increasing trader confidence and usability.
